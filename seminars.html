Online Seminars

Research presentations from active scholars across disciplines

Direct Access to Ongoing Scholarship

Our seminar series features researchers sharing their current work, its motivation, and its broader context. Talks follow a familiar online-seminar format and highlight how ideas develop, what challenges arise, and where the research is heading. The series offers students and peers direct exposure to ongoing scholarship in a clear and approachable form.

Upcoming & Recent Seminars

Agentic AI Systems for Scientific Discovery: Challenges and Opportunities

Dr. Sarah Chen, MIT | December 20, 2024 at 2:00 PM EST

This talk explores the development of multi-agent AI systems designed to accelerate scientific discovery across computational biology and materials science. Dr. Chen will discuss the architecture of autonomous research agents, their integration with existing scientific workflows, and early results from deployments in drug discovery and protein engineering. The presentation will cover both technical challenges—such as ensuring reliability and interpretability—and broader questions about how AI tools reshape the practice of experimental science.
